## What We're Building

A **By-Name List coordination system** connecting homeless service providers in Santa Fe through secure, HIPAA-compliant data sharing.

**The Problem:** Providers operate in silos without knowing what services individuals are receiving, leading to duplication and missed opportunities.

**Our Solution:** Real-time coordination platform enabling providers to share appropriate information, coordinate care, and track progress toward housing stability.
